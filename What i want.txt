Design Principles
Lots of whitespace
2-3 colors maximum
2 font family (Inter, Geist ya Satoshi)
Thin borders instead of heavy shadows
Smooth micro animations only
No unnecessary gradients
High quality typography

Ab mujhe tumhara vision clear ho gaya hai. Aur honestly mujhe lagta hai tum **Sebastian + Juan Mora ko mix** nahi karna chahiye, balki **Sebastian ko base** rakhna chahiye aur sirf **Juan Mora ka folder interaction** lena chahiye.

Jo folder screenshot tumne bheja hai, usme idea bohot unique hai: user ko lagta hai woh files browse kar raha hai, na ke sirf cards dekh raha hai. Ye interaction memorable hai, lekin baaki website minimal rehni chahiye.

---

# Design Philosophy

## **Quiet. Confident. Intentional.**

Website dekh kar aisa feel aaye:

> "Ye designer apni skills ko shout nahi kar raha, uska kaam khud bol raha hai."

Isliye har cheez ka purpose hoga.

* No unnecessary gradients
* No floating shapes
* No glassmorphism
* No random animations
* No parallax overload
* No hero video

Bas clean typography, whitespace aur premium interactions.

## 🔥 Meri ek aur suggestion (jo mujhe lagta hai website ko next level le jayegi)

Work section me sirf folders mat rakho. Unhe **operating system style** feel do.

```
📁 Restaurants
gallery + direction 
    ├── Bella's Restaurant
    ├── Bite Me
    └── Pizza House

📁 Business
gallery + direction 
    ├── Landing Page
    ├── AI Studio
    └── Dashboard

📁 Personal
gallery + direction 
    ├── Portfolio
    └── Branding
```

User jab folder khole to gallery appear ho, aur kisi project par click karte hi dedicated case study page khul jaye. Is tarah tumhara portfolio ek normal showcase nahi lagega, balki ek curated design archive jaisa feel dega—clean, memorable aur premium, bina unnecessary visual noise ke. Yehi tumhari website ki unique identity ban sakti hai.
